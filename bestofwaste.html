Best Out Of Waste

The Best out of Waste event at Avazya techfest is a creative and eco-friendly event that challenges participants to repurpose waste materials and turn them into something useful and beautiful. The event is designed to promote sustainability and encourage participants to think creatively about ways to reduce waste.

PARTICIPANT: Team / Individual

TEMPLATE:
To maximize the effectiveness of your model, please consider the following:
1. Dimensions: Small or Big Models.
2. Style: Informatic and Attractive.
3. Material used : Recycling materials.
4. Acknowledgements: should include anybody who has helped you
5. A handout: It is a good idea to provide interest among viewers with Attractiveness of your model .

TOPIC:
1. New Inventions
2. New Technologies
3. General Topics
4. Social Awareness
5. Health/Science
6. Etc.

RULES:
• Bring your own Materials
• Explanation can be done VERBAL.
• 1 hour minimum preparation and Maximum 1 hour 30 Minutes.
• Team / individual.
• Jury’s decision is based on the various aspects and is final.

REGISTRATION FEE : Free.
DATE: 1st April 2023
EVENT TIME: 10:00 TO 2.00
During the presentation, the participant will have a limited amount of time, typically between 5 to 10 minutes.
