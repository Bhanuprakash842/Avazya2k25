Welcome To
AVAZYA 2025

AVAZYA is a spectacular National Level Tech Fest organized by CMREC (CMR Engineering College)
On 11th April ,2025

Project Expo

The Project Expo event at Avazya techfest is a showcase of the most innovative and cutting-edge projects from students in the fields of technology and engineering. Participants will display their projects and prototypes, highlighting the research, development, and design that went into creating them. Overall, the Project Expo event at Avazya techfest is a platform for students to showcase their projects, receive valuable feedback, and gain exposure to potential employers and industry experts. We're excited to see the exciting and groundbreaking projects that will be on display at this year's techfest!

Poster / Idea Presentation

The poster presentation event at AVAZYA is an exciting opportunity for students to showcase their research and projects in a visually engaging format. Participants will design a poster that highlights their work in technology and engineering, using images, graphs, and diagrams to communicate their findings. Overall, the poster presentation event is an exciting way for students to showcase their work, improve their communication skills, and connect with industry experts. We're looking forward to seeing the creativity and innovation on display at this year's techfest!

Paper Presentation

Paper Presentation is an event where the participants are required to present on any topic of interest, in papers. The goal of a paper presentation event is to provide a platform for researchers to share their findings, ideas, and insights with a broader audience, and to receive feedback and suggestions from peers and experts in their field. These events can be valuable opportunities for participants to gain exposure, build networks, and receive recognition for their work.

Code Tech

Code Tech is a thrilling event that will take place at the Avazya techfest, which consists of various rounds of quiz, debugging, and coding challenges. The event is designed to test the coding and technical skills of participants and challenge them to think critically and innovatively.

JAM , Debate &
Group discussions

At Avazya techfest 2023, we're excited to host a range of engaging and thought-provoking events, including JAM, debate, and group discussion. These events are designed to foster critical thinking, communication skills, and teamwork among participants. These events are open to all students, regardless of major or background. Reel Making

A ReelMaking competition is being conducted as part of a tech fest. The competition is open to participants who wish to showcase their creativity and Reelmaking skills. Participants can submit Reels on any topic related to technology, innovation, or science fiction. The Reels can be in any language and should not exceed a specified duration. The competition provides a platform for aspiring filmmakers to showcase their talent and creativity in the field of technology and innovation.

Treasure Hunt

Avazya Tech-Fest is excited to announce a quiz and Treasurehunt competition that will challenge the participants' knowledge and skills in various fields of technology. This quiz competition will provide an opportunity for tech enthusiasts to showcase their expertise and win exciting prizes. The competition is open to all tech enthusiasts, and the participants can register themselves online. The event promises to be exciting and informative, and we encourage everyone to participate and have a chance to win exciting prizes.

Best Out Of Waste

The Best out of Waste event at Avazya techfest is a creative and eco-friendly event that challenges participants to repurpose waste materials and turn them into something useful and beautiful. The event is designed to promote sustainability and encourage participants to think creatively about ways to reduce waste.
