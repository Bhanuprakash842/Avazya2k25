Short film

A short film competition is being conducted as part of a tech fest. The competition is open to participants who wish to showcase their creativity and filmmaking skills. Participants can submit short films on any topic related to technology, innovation, or science fiction. The films can be in any language and should not exceed a specified duration. A panel of judges comprising of experts in the film industry will evaluate the entries and select the winners based on criteria such as originality, creativity, technical excellence, and storytelling. The winners will receive cash prizes, certificates, and an opportunity to showcase their films at the tech fest. The competition provides a platform for aspiring filmmakers to showcase their talent and creativity in the field of technology and innovation.

RULES AND REGULATIONS OF SHORT FILM MAKING
• Short film duration should be less than 20 minutes.
• Short film must be theme based and should pass a message.
• In short film, consumption of alcohol and drugs are prohibited.
• Adult content is also strictly prohibited.
• Do not copy from youtube or any OTT platfrom
• Should be submitted on time (i.e 29th March 2023).
